How To Play

You're a cunning theif breaking into the mansion of Mr. Albert, a forgetful yet brilliant Mathematician.


His safes are rumored to hold priceless diamomds, but each is locked with a code protected by math based riddles.


Luckily for you, Albert left behind clues to help himself remember the codes.




1. Players must solve mathematical puzzles to unlock a "lock" within a limited number of attempts


2. Players are allowed a fixed number of attempts per level (3)


3. Each guess/attempt must be a 5-digit code.


4. Are you ready to unlock new skills, and uncover the secret codes? Lets begin your journey!
